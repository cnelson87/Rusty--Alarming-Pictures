About Us

Founded in 2001, the company consists of Jack and Carol Hodge. Brother and sister and not married.

Their diverse talents range from directing award winning spots (the integrated campaign for Rainier Beer alone won AICP/Ad Age’s inaugural Battle of the Brands, a gold Content & Contact Clio, a gold in the Hybrid category of the New York Art Director’s Club and the National Best of Show at the Addys) to shooting and scoring beautiful mini-films for JCrew in far flung locations (Paris, Patagonia, Rome...).

Alarming Pictures has a short attention span, boyish enthusiasm, and the ability to make a good strong cocktail and nice 3 layer cake.
